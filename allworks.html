About Me

I am a believer in Jesus Christ, and I strive to reflect His character in all I do. I am committed to child safeguarding, advocacy for children and families, and leadership development. I work with caregivers, teachers, and leaders to strengthen safeguarding systems through awareness and training. Through Safe Steps Training Services, the end goal is to protect children, empower families and strengthen communities. I design programs that create safe environments where children can grow and thrive.
